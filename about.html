This Site's Purpose

This site will display Menu and catering items for the restaurant, The Copper Kettle (CK). The index page will have a basic layout with one of the reviews from the Battle Creek Enquirer with a link to the fill review. The menu page will present all of the menu items listed at the CK, along with subpages for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The catering page will have all of the information about the catering service provided by the CK. The reservations page will include a form that the user will be able to fill out to reserve a table at the CK.
